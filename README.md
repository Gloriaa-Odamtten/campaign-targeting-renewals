# Optimizing Campaign Targeting for Policy Renewals

## Project Overview
This project focuses on improving policy renewal campaigns for an insurance company using customer household demographics and purchase behavior.

The goal is to identify customers most likely to respond to renewal offers and help the marketing team run more targeted, cost-effective campaigns.

**Company:** OptiSecure Insurance Ltd (UK)  
**Industry:** General Insurance  
**Tools Used:** Google Sheets, Looker Studio  

---

## Business Problem
The company experienced:
- Declining renewal conversion rates
- High marketing spend with low returns
- Poor personalization in campaigns
- Difficulty predicting customer response

This resulted in reduced customer retention and revenue loss.

---

## Project Objectives
1. Segment customers using household & behavioral data  
2. Identify key drivers of campaign response  
3. Build a customer scoring model  
4. Visualize insights in dashboards  
5. Provide data-driven recommendations

## Methodology
### Data Processing
- Data cleaning in Google Sheets
- Handling missing values
- Feature engineering

### Analysis
- Customer segmentation
- Behavioral analysis
- Response pattern identification

### Scoring Model
- Developed a rule-based scoring model
- Ranked customers by likelihood to respond

### Visualization
- Interactive dashboards in Looker Studio
- Segment performance tracking
- Campaign ROI insights

---

## Key Insights
- High-income households respond better
- Customers with recent purchases are more likely to renew
- Wine and online shoppers show higher engagement
- Families with children show moderate response
